Common questions

FAQ — answered
honestly

Are these prices real — what's the catch?

No catch. We're a new studio building our reputation. Lower prices now means more reviews and a stronger portfolio. You benefit from that directly.

How long does a project actually take?

Starter landing pages: 5–7 days. Full websites: 10–14 days. Web apps: 3–4 weeks. We give you a firm deadline before starting and we stick to it.

Do I own the code after launch?

100%. Once payment is received, all code, assets, and design files are yours. No ongoing fees, no lock-in, ever.

How does the 50% founding discount work?

The first 10 clients who contact us get 50% off any plan — just tell us you're a founding client and we apply the discount. No complex conditions.

What do you need from me to get started?

A brief about your business, any inspiration sites you like, your logo if you have one, and the content you want. We guide you through everything else.

Do you work with clients outside Sri Lanka?

Absolutely. We work remotely with clients worldwide. PayPal, bank transfer, or Wise. Time zones are never a problem — we respond fast.

What if I need changes after launch?

Growth and Professional plans include 30–90 days post-launch support. After that, affordable hourly or retainer options are available.

Can you build the backend too?

Yes. Our Professional plan includes a full Node.js + PostgreSQL backend with admin dashboard, auth, and API. We also offer backend-only projects.

How we work

A process designed
for clarity

01
Discovery & brief
We start with a focused conversation about your business, goals, and target audience. No lengthy questionnaires — just a clear understanding of what you need, what success looks like, and what timeline works for you. This sets the foundation for everything that follows.
02
Design & wireframe
Before a single line of code is written, we produce high-fidelity Figma designs so you see exactly what you're getting. You review, request changes, and give sign-off — only then do we move forward. No surprises, no wasted development time.
03
Build & iterate
We build in focused sprints with regular check-ins so you always know where things stand. Clean, semantic, performant code — no bloat, no shortcuts. Every component is crafted with scalability and maintainability in mind from day one.
04
Test & optimise
We run thorough cross-browser testing, mobile responsiveness checks, Lighthouse performance audits, and on-page SEO validation before anything goes live. We don't ship until every metric meets production standards — not just demo standards.
05
Launch & support
We handle deployment to your chosen platform — Vercel, AWS, or your own hosting — and remain fully available post-launch. Growth and Professional plans include dedicated support windows. The relationship doesn't end at delivery; it's just getting started.
